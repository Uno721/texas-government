Welcome

The Office of the Governor serves as the chief executive authority of the State of Texas. The Governor oversees the executive branch, works alongside the legislature, appoints key officials, and ensures that the laws of Texas are faithfully executed.

This website provides official information regarding the structure, duties, and operations of Texas state government.
