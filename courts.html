Texas Court System

Texas Supreme Court

The Texas Supreme Court is the highest civil court in the state. It oversees civil appeals, constitutional issues, and statewide legal precedent.

Texas Court of Criminal Appeals

The Court of Criminal Appeals is the highest authority on criminal cases in Texas, handling serious criminal appeals and death penalty cases.

Courts of Appeal

Intermediate appellate courts that review decisions from district and county courts to ensure legal accuracy and fairness.
